1. Place this folder to your Public_html in your host.
2. Change line #5 (Replace it with your chat_id/user_id, you can also set ID of a group (But you need to add bot into the group first!) Also you can get your user_id account with bot (@UserInfoBot).
3. Change line #6 (Replace it with your Bot TOKEN!) you can get your bot token here (@BotFather).
4. Set webhook (If you don't know how, the nsearch ut in google!)
5. Start your bot, its DONE!
